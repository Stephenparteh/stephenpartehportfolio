My Experience

Intern Full Stack Developer at Orange Digital Center Liberia

July 2024 - September 2024

Collaborated with a multidisciplinary team, including Embedded Engineers, Front-End Developers, UI Designers, and other developers, on the "Forever Low-Cost Option for Water" (FLOW) project for the "Orange Summer Challenge." The project aimed to make safe water accessible and affordable to every household in Liberia. The team developed a system that receives real-time data from IoT components, enabling households to monitor water usage and make payments via Orange Money. An admin dashboard was also created to manage users and households, ensuring seamless control of the entire system.
Successfully integrated IoT data and payment systems, enhancing user transparency and administrative control.
**Technologies Used:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, SQL, EJS.
